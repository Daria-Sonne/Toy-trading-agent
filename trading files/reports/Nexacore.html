Letter from the CEO

Dear Shareholders,

2025 has been a transformational year for NexaCore Technologies. We've achieved unprecedented growth across all our business segments, solidifying our position as a leader in the global technology landscape. Our strategic investments in artificial intelligence, cloud infrastructure, and quantum computing have yielded exceptional returns, both in terms of revenue growth and technological advancement.

I am pleased to report that we delivered record-breaking financial results with a revenue of $289.4 billion, representing a remarkable 27.8% increase from the previous year. Our operating margin expanded to an impressive 36.9%, reflecting our commitment to operational excellence and innovation-driven efficiency.

The success of our AI-powered cloud services platform, NexaCloud, has exceeded our most optimistic projections, with a customer base that has grown by 68% year-over-year. Our quantum computing division has made breakthrough advancements that have positioned us at the forefront of this revolutionary technology, attracting significant interest from both commercial and governmental partners.

As we look ahead to 2026, we remain dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in technology. Our robust research and development pipeline promises exciting new products and services that we believe will continue to drive substantial growth and create long-term value for our shareholders.

Thank you for your continued trust and investment in NexaCore Technologies.

Dr. Elena J. Nakamura
Founder and Chief Executive Officer

Outlook for 2026

Based on our current assessment of market conditions, technology roadmap, and operational plans, we provide the following guidance for fiscal year 2026:

  • Revenue growth of 20-25% year-over-year
  • Operating margin expansion of 100-150 basis points
  • Capital expenditure of $45-50 billion, focused on AI and quantum computing infrastructure
  • R&D investments of $55-60 billion, representing approximately 18-19% of projected revenue
  • NexaCloud & AI Services segment expected to grow 40-45%
  • Continued commitment to strategic acquisitions in emerging technologies
  • Launch of next-generation quantum processors with 2,048+ qubits
