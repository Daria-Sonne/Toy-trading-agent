Letter from the CEO

Dear Shareholders,

2025 has been a year of strategic resilience for PetroGlobal Inc. In a constantly evolving global energy landscape, we've maintained our commitment to operational excellence and financial stability, while advancing our investments in sustainable practices and technologies.

Despite global market fluctuations and increasing regulatory pressures, our core business operations have delivered consistent results, validating our balanced approach to growth and risk management. We achieved a revenue of $143.7 billion, representing a modest 3.2% increase from the previous year, while maintaining our industry-leading operating margin of 22.8%.

We continue to prioritize shareholder value, increasing our dividend for the 17th consecutive year, while making calculated investments in our future. Our dedication to responsible resource development and strategic diversification has allowed us to navigate complex market dynamics while preserving our financial strength.

As we look ahead to 2026, we remain committed to our core values of operational excellence, financial discipline, and sustainable development. We are confident in our ability to deliver long-term value for our shareholders while adapting to the evolving global energy transition.

Thank you for your continued trust and investment in PetroGlobal Inc.

Richard H. Thornton
Chairman and Chief Executive Officer

Outlook for 2026

Based on our current assessment of market conditions and operational plans, we provide the following guidance for fiscal year 2026:

  • Capital expenditure of $18-20 billion
  • Production growth of 1-3%
  • Operating margin target of 22-24%
  • Continued commitment to shareholder returns through dividends and share repurchases
  • Increased investments in low-carbon technologies with planned expenditures of $5.5-6.0 billion
